# 💎 Prism Ulto

**Prism Ulto** is a hyper-minimalist, high-performance productivity dashboard that replaces your default Chrome New Tab page. Designed for focus and efficiency, it brings your essential tools into a beautiful, Glassmorphism-inspired interface.

Built with **SOLID principles** and a modular ES6 architecture, Prism Ulto is as clean under the hood as it looks on your screen.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🖥️ Mac-Style Dock
A centered, fluid dock at the bottom of your screen inspired by macOS. 
- **Smart Shortcuts**: One-click access to your favorite sites (Gmail, Gemini, Leetcode, etc.).
- **Dynamic Hover**: Smooth scaling animations and floating tooltips.
- **Glassmorphism**: Beautifully blurred background that adapts to any wallpaper.

### 🔍 Multi-Engine Search
A powerful search bar at the top-left that gives you total control.
- **Provider Selector**: Switch instantly between **Google, Bing, and DuckDuckGo**.
- **Visual Identity**: Icons update dynamically based on your selected engine.
- **Quick Access**: Press `/` to focus the search bar from anywhere.

### ⏱️ Persistent Study Timer
A Pomodoro-style timer that follows you across tabs.
- **Tab Consistency**: Uses `localStorage` and target-time syncing so your timer never resets when you open a new tab.
- **Dual Modes**: Toggle between **Study** and **Rest** intervals.

### 📅 Google Ecosystem Integration
Full, secure integration with your Google account.
- **Google Calendar**: View your upcoming 5 events at a glance.
- **Google Tasks**: Manage your to-do list directly from the dashboard.
- **Smart Identity**: Displays your Google profile and a dynamic time-based greeting.

### 📝 Productivity Tools
- **Daily Focus**: Set your primary goal for the day and keep it front-and-center.
- **Quick Notes**: A slide-out panel for instant thoughts, saved automatically.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Seamlessly adapts to your preferred aesthetic.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

Prism Ulto is built for maintainability. The codebase is fully modular:

- **`js/core/`**: Centralized state management.
- **`js/services/`**: Abstracted storage and authentication logic.
- **`js/components/`**: Decoupled, logic-independent UI components.

---

## 🚀 Installation

1.  Download or clone this repository.
2.  Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  Enable **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
4.  Click **Load unpacked** and select the Prism Ulto folder.
