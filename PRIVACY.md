# Privacy Policy for Prism Ulto

**Official URL**: https://suren-dev.web.app/prism-ulto/privacy-policy
**Last Updated: April 26, 2026**

Your privacy is a priority. **Prism Ulto** is designed to be a private, local-first productivity tool. This policy explains how we handle your data.

---

## 1. Data Collection & Usage
Prism Ulto **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal information to external servers. All your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your own device.

*   **Local Storage**: Your daily focus, quick notes, search provider preferences, and study timer state are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking scripts to monitor your behavior.

## 2. Google Integration (OAuth)
Prism Ulto offers optional integration with Google services (Calendar and Tasks) to enhance your productivity.
*   **Authentication**: When you "Connect Google," the extension requests an OAuth token via Google's official identity API.
*   **Data Access**: The extension uses this token to fetch your upcoming calendar events and task lists.
*   **Processing**: This data is fetched directly from Google's APIs and rendered in your browser. It is **never** sent to the developer or any third party.
*   **Storage**: Access tokens are handled securely by the Chrome browser and are not stored in our own databases.

## 3. Search Functionality
When you use the search bar, your query is sent directly to the search provider you have selected (Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo). Prism Ulto does not see, log, or store your search queries.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Prism Ulto may display favicons for your quick links using Google's favicon service (`s2/favicons`). This is a standard practice and involves sending only the domain name of the link to fetch the icon.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in functionality. Any updates will be posted here.
